Welcome to the Cute Services static preview

This static demo shows the basic layout for the Owner, Staff, and Reseller portals. For full functionality (pick/pass, chat, uploads, etc.), the live app needs a backend.

  • The Owner portal displays metrics and settings.
  • The Staff portal allows your team to pick orders, chat, and fulfill them.
  • The Reseller portal is where your reseller and their team submit orders and view balances.
